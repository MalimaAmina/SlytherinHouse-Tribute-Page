Slytherin House

Founded at the time of all the great houses, Slytherin House; aptly named after its founder Salazar Slytherin. Slytherin house embodies only the most necessary qualities of its namesake and alumni; Cunning, Ambition, Leadership and Resourcefulness. It is said that some of the most talented but also dangerous wizards and witches once found a home at Slytherin House, Hogwarts.
